Manual Currency Exchange Rate

This Module Allows Manual Currency Rate in Sale,Purchase,Invoice/Bill

Invoice

Change Currency in the invoice then click on Apply Manual Currency. Enter currency exchange rate. So price unit of that product will be converted to selected currency with exchange rate

Journal Entry

Journal entry will be in company currency





Sale

Change the price list in other currency then click on Apply Manual Currency. Enter currency exchange rate. So price unit of that product will be converted to selected currency with exchange rate

Purchase

Change Currency in the Purchase then click on Apply Manual Currency. Enter currency exchange rate. So price unit of that product will be converted to selected currency with exchange rate
